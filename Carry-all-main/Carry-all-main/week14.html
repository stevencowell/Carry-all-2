Week 14: Peer Feedback & Final Refinements

Giving and Receiving Peer Feedback

  • Positives first – highlight what works well
  • Specific suggestions – be clear and constructive
  • Focus on function and finish – keep feedback task-oriented

When receiving feedback, listen openly, ask clarifying questions, and decide which suggestions to implement.

Refining Your Project

  • Sand any rough patches or missed corners
  • Re-align or tighten hardware
  • Touch up finish drips or blemishes
  • Add final details, like labels or padding

Professional Presentation

  • Ensure all parts are clean and secure
  • Remove pencil marks and glue spots
  • Consider adding a name tag or brief reflection
